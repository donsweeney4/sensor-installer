Device Installer

Automatically install or update the software on your TVYCAP temperature sensor.

Works on browsers Chrome, Edge, Opera and Brave. Does not work on Safari or Firefox.

Connect your ESP32-C6 temperature sensor via USB cable and click the button below. Some cheap USB cables only support charging. Use a normal USB cable that supports data and charging. You'll be asked to select the serial port where your sensor is connected. If you have several choices, you can just try various ports until you find the one that works.
